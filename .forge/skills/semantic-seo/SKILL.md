# Semantic SEO Evaluation Skill
# SemanticPipe v1.1 (generic — site-agnostic)

Evaluate and improve articles across 8 dimensions.

## The 8 Dimensions (score 1-5 each)

### 1. Entity Coverage
Named people, places, institutions, events.
- 1: <3 entities | 2: 3-5 generic | 3: 5-10 some specific | 4: 10-15 domain-specific | 5: 15+ comprehensive

### 2. Temporal Depth
Specific dates, years, chronological grounding.
- 1: none | 2: 1-2 vague | 3: 3-5 specific years | 4: 6-10 with context | 5: 10+ clear narrative

### 3. Source Authority
Named sources, citations, evidence.
- 1: none | 2: 1 vague | 3: 2-3 named | 4: 4-6 with findings | 5: 7+ including primary

### 4. Topical Breadth
H2 diversity covering distinct subtopics.
- 1: <3 H2s repetitive | 2: 3-4 narrow | 3: 4-5 main angles | 4: 6-7 comprehensive | 5: 8+ exhaustive

### 5. Data Density
Numbers, measurements, statistics, percentages.
- 1: none | 2: 1-2 vague | 3: 3-5 specific | 4: 6-10 contextualized | 5: 10+ with structure

### 6. AEO (Answer Engine Optimization)
Extractability by snippets, AI answer boxes, PAA.
- 1: no signals | 2: vague opener | 3: decent opener + 2 Q-heads | 4: strong + definitions + lists | 5: perfect AEO

### 7. Internal Linking
Silo structure, hub connections, cross-links.
- 1: 0-1 links | 2: 2 no hub | 3: 3+ with hub | 4: 4-6 proper silo | 5: comprehensive

### 8. Evidence Hedging
Confidence calibration — fact vs tradition/claim.
- 1: all fact | 2: 1 hedge | 3: 2-3 hedges | 4: consistent | 5: every uncertain claim qualified

## Grades
- **A (36-40):** Publication-ready
- **B (28-35):** Solid, minor improvements
- **C (20-27):** Needs 2-3 dimensions improved
- **D (<20):** Major rewrite

## Process
1. Read full article → 2. Score each dimension → 3. Calculate total → 4. Identify 2-3 weakest → 5. Those become optimization targets → 6. Log evaluation
